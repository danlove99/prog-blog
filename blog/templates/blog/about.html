About Me

My journey so far

Hi, I'm a hobbiest developer and professional computer technician that's passionate about DL/ML models implemented into web applications. I also strongly agree with the views of the free software Foundation and am a devoted Linux user.

My programming language of choice is Python but I use C++ when experimenting with both microcontrollers and microprocessors, you can see some of my recent and current robotics projects on the *showcase* section of this site along with some recent Python projects.

The blog section consists of both posts keeping up with my learning progress and educational posts about topics that I'm already knowledgeable about. I hope to one day become a professional developer but I am very much doing this for personal satisfaction at the moment rather than for any financial gain. When I'm not working on any projects I like to contribute to the open source community on Github. Thanks for viewing my about me and I hope you enjoy my site.
